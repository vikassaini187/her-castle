== Description ==
**Get More Visitors & Leads From Your WordPress Website**

With dsIDXpress, you can easily add SEO-friendly MLS listings to your WordPress website to attract more visitors.

Use shortcodes, widgets, and custom pages to display powerful MLS search tools that will keep visitors on your website looking at homes for sale.

Lead capture tools, like forms for scheduling showings and saving property searches, will help you turn these visitors into leads. Daily or weekly listing alerts will assist with converting your leads into clients.

Why dsIDXpress is the right IDX solution for your website:
*   Detailed, up-to-date listings: Listing details pulled straight from your MLS's IDX feed, plus information about local schools, lists of similar and recently-sold homes, and bird's-eye and street view maps.
*   Powerful MLS search widgets: Visitors can easily find MLS listings on your website with powerful map search widgets, slideshows of highlighted properties, and more.
*   Lead capture tools: Turn visitors into leads with forms for scheduling showings, website registration, saving searches, and contacting you for more information.
*   Search engine friendly: Bring in more visitors from search engines with unique indexable listing pages, Google XML sitemaps, and optimized page titles.
*   Install and set up in minutes: Installing and setting up the plugin is fast and simple, regardless of your web development experience. 
*   Optimized for mobile devices: The plugin's mobile-friendly design ensures smartphone-using visitors will have a good experience on your website.
*   Extensively customizable: Easily customize IDX listing details and the styling of MLS search forms and widgets.
*   Integrated contact management: A built-in CRM stores, organizes, and tracks your leads and shows their saved searches, favorite listings, and listing search history.

== Installation ==
Install dsIDXpress on your website like you would install any other WordPress plugin.

**Install via the WordPress admin area:**
1. In the WordPress admin area, visit "Plugins" > "Add New."
2. Search for "dsIDXpress" using the search bar.
3. Click "Install Now," and then "Activate Plugin."
4. See the "Activation" section below.

**Install by uploading the plugin:**
1. Upload the "dsidxpress" folder to your website's "wp-content/plugins" directory. 
2. Activate the plugin by visiting the "Plugins" menu in the WordPress admin area.
3. See the "Activation" section below.

**ACTIVATION**

Once installed, dsIDXpress must be activated by entering the activation key for your free trial or paid subscription. Simply visit the "IDX" page linked to in the WordPress admin area sidebar and enter the activation key into the text box.
